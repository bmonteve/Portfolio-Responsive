About Me

My name is Brian Monteverde. I am currently 24 years old living in San Diego, California. I graduated from the University of California San Diego with a Bachelors of Science for Biochemistry and Cell Biology. I currently work the Scripps Research Institute doing Flow Cytometry. I work hand in hand with researchers sorting specific populations of cells based on their fluorescence for labeled antibodies. Currently I am taking classes for coding and web development to enhance my background in the coding world and gain a new subset of skills that can be applied to a possible career change.
